### About

I am a PhD student in the [Isborn Group](https://sites.ucmerced.edu/cisborn/) at the
**University of California, Merced**, supervised by **Dr. Christine Isborn**. My research
centers on **computing excitonic couplings of chromophores in complex environments** — the
electronic interactions that govern electronic energy transfer (EET) in light-harvesting
systems and organic optical materials.

I hold a **BS-MS Dual Degree** in Chemical Sciences from the **Indian Institute of Science
Education and Research (IISER) Berhampur**, Odisha, India.

### Research Interests

- **Excited-State Electronic Structure** : TD-DFT, excitonic coupling, electronic energy transfer
- **Environmental Effects on Spectroscopy** : QM/MM, solvation, absorption/emission line-shape modeling
- **Molecular Dynamics** : conformational and environmental sampling for spectral simulation
- **Machine Learning in Chemistry** : neural-network prediction of molecular and excited-state properties
